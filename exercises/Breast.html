Bench Press

Before we discuss about adjusting and enhancing the move, let's go through the fundamentals. On a bench, lie flat on your back. Grip the bar with hands little wider than shoulder width apart, such that your hands are precisely above your elbows when you're at the bottom of the motion. This enables for the greatest amount of force to be generated. As you breathe in, carefully lower the bar to your chest. Push up as you exhale, firmly grasping the bar and keeping an eye on a location on the ceiling rather than the bar to ensure it follows the same course each time.

Standing Cable Fly

"Begin by facing your palms outward. Bring your hands to your sternum (the middle of your chest), squeeze for two seconds, and then gently return your hands to your sides." Rep for a total of three sets of 10 reps. This exercise is frequently performed improperly in the gym, leading to underdeveloped pecs and overdeveloped anterior deltoids. When you're attempting to move a lot of weight, you could end up with bad form that hits the wrong muscle group.
